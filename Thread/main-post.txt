[size=6][b]FIONA FEATS FAMILIARS TRACKER[/b][/size][/center]

If you're looking for a compact(-ish) list of all familiars needed for Fiona feats, you've come to the right place! This is primarily intended as a template you can put in a dragon bio or a forum post to keep track of which familiars you've already obtained or awakended, but you can also just... look at it here if that's useful to you I guess.

The code is available in two versions, one is intended for forum posts and one for dragon bios, but you're free to edit both of them as much as you want to suit your needs. I use [i]italics[/i] for obtained familiars and [s][i]italics+strike-through[/i][/s] for awakened ones in my [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/forums/qnc/3416971/1#post_58988457]personal tracking thread[/url], but you can use whatever works for you. You also don't have to keep the credit I included in the template if you don't want to!

If you spot any mistakes, have any suggestions, or notice that the template has not been updated for a newly released feat, feel free to ping me in this thread or send me a PM. [emoji=new friend size=1]

[b]If you missed one or several past updates of the template and don't want to fill in a new copy, check [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/forums/gde/3416901/1#post_58979492]this post[/url] for an archive of update instructions![/b]
(Do note that the date format used for version numbers is [b]dd-mm-yyyy[/b], [i]not[/i] mm-dd-yyyy.)

Check out [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/forums/gde/2835296]this thread[/url] by Kapow for a less compact but much more detailed list.
For festival and coliseum boss familiars, see [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/forums/gde/3432578]this template[/url] by puppybeast!
